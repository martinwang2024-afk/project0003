FAQ

What is pigeon genetic testing?

DNA analysis that detects variants linked to racing traits.

How accurate is pigeon DNA testing?

High for genotype calls with quality laboratory processes.

Which genes influence racing performance?

LDHA, CRY1, DRD4, MSTN, LRP8, GSR, CASK and F-KER are commonly evaluated.

How do breeders interpret genotype results?

By comparing favorable combinations and balancing multi-trait outcomes.

How does probability prediction work?

Mendelian inheritance and Punnett squares estimate offspring frequencies.

How to improve breeding success?

Pair compatible birds, maintain diversity, and validate with race outcomes.

Can this tool run offline?
